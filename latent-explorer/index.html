Latent Explorer

Explore the latent space of the Stable Audio 3 autoencoder — entirely in your browser. Encode sounds into 256-dim latents (~10.8 per second), then morph, sculpt and hand-conduct them back into audio.

Model

Weights download once and are cached. The SAME-L conversion was made from the stable-audio-3-medium checkpoint for this app; the alternative is stable-audio-morph-onnx.

Hand control 🖐

Conduct the latent space with your hand via webcam (MediaPipe hand landmarks, processed locally). Move left–right to scrub through the sound; height, openness and pinch steer latent directions. Audio loops a short latent window and re-decodes continuously.
