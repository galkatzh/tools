Stable Audio SFX

Generate sound effects from text with Stable Audio 3 Small SFX. Runs entirely in your browser — accelerated by WASM (CPU) when available.

First run downloads ~615 MB of model weights (cached afterwards; the text encoder and decoder are shared with the Stable Audio music app, so those are reused if already cached). The diffusion runs on WebGPU where supported, otherwise on the CPU.
